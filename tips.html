Cooking Tips

  1. Use two bowls to cut corn off the cob without getting kernels everywhere.
  2. Pre-soak pasta and it will cook in about 60 seconds.
  3. Instantly core a head of iceberg lettuce by slamming it down on a cutting board.
  4. Slice avocadoes right in the skin to make neat cubes.
  5. Put a dish towel or damp paper towel under your cutting board to keep it from slipping and wobbling.
  6. Use a spoon to peel a kiwi from the inside out.
  7. Grate frozen butter straight into pastry dough for fast, even distribution.
  8. Hold cherry tomatoes between two plastic lids to slice them all at once.
  9. Caramelize onions in half the time by adding a little baking soda.
  10. Microwave an ear of corn and it'll fall right out of the husk.
